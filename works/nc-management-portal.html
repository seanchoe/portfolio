In February 2015, ZeroDesktop acquired core technology assets from NComputing. NComputing is a desktop virtualization company with more than 70,000 customers in 140 countries.

I started working on designing NComputing's latest management software family. Management Portal is a part of it, and it lets users to manage their software licenses and thin client devices they have purchased on the web.

vSpace Manager is a software which customers install on their host computers to manage all of the connected thin client devices.
