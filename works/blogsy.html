Blogsy is a Blogging app for iPad.

We made it from our own needs. When we had our iPads for the first time, we wanted a nice, easy-to-use blogging app for iPad. But we couldn't find one, so we started to develop Blogsy.

It once had been ranked 32nd total, and 2nd top grossing app in social networking category in U.S. App Store.

I designed the whole UI and developed the most UI part of the code.

We had a mascot for Blogsy called Fomo. Every major update we did, we put a name of a mythical creature and added an image of Fomo in it's shape.
