Blogsy is a blogging app for iPad.

In Fomola, we made Blogsy for our own needs. When our team had our iPads for the first time, we wanted a nice, easy-to-use blogging app for iPad. But we couldn't find one, so we started to develop Blogsy.

It once had been ranked 32nd top paid app in U.S. App Store, and also 2nd top grossing app in social networking category.

We had a mascot for Blogsy called Fomo. For every major update we did, we gave it a name of a mythical creature and added an image of Fomo in it's shape.
