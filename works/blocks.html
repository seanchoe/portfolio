Blocks is an iOS app that can help you controlling children's smart phone usage. Parents can monitor child's activities from their own phone.

Once the parent initiate a Instant or Scheduled Goal, The child receives a notification. Once the goal is started, if the child open the device during the set period, the goal fails. If they don't use the device until the goal finishes, they earn the Blocks as reward.

Instead of forcing to stop using the phone, our approach was to give positive reward to children if they use their phones less. Initially we designed it to give "Trophies" as rewards to children, which are like virtual stickers.

However when we did a survey with these trophy designs, our customers gave us feedbacks that their children are not motivated by them. So we have decided to change them to real-life rewards that parents can set for their children.

Blocks has not been released to App Store yet.
